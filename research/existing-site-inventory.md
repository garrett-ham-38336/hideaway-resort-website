- Fully equipped kitchens in all units

## 7. Activities & Attractions

### On-Site / Table Rock Lake
- Bass fishing (Top 100 Bass Lakes nationally)
- Boating and sailing
- Wakeboarding and water skiing
- Paddleboarding
- Swimming
- Cliff diving
- Scenic sunset viewing

### Table Rock Lake Facts
- Over 850 miles of shoreline
- 43,000+ acres of surface area
- Water depths exceeding 200 feet
- Fishable year-round (mild winters)

### Branson, MO (20–30 min drive)
- Silver Dollar City (18 miles away)
- 76 Strip (20 miles away)
- Live entertainment shows
- Shopping (outlets and craft stores)
- Championship golf courses
- Hiking and biking trails
- Christmas in Branson / Ozark Mountain Christmas (seasonal)
- Airports: Branson Airport (BKG), Springfield-Branson National Airport (SGF)
- Resource: ExploreBranson.com

### Boating Regulations
- Missouri boater education law: anyone born after Jan 1, 1984 must pass a Missouri Boater Education course
- Hideaway Marina next door offers rentals (fishing boats, sport boats, pontoons, paddleboards, jet skis)

### Fishing Licensing
- Missouri: valid license required (exceptions: residents 65+, anyone under 15)
- Arkansas side: similar licensing rules
- Online license acquisition available
