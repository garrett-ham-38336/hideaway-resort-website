## 6. Policies

Source: Guesty "Other Things to Note" descriptions (consistent across listings).

- **Minimum age:** Must be 21 or older to book
- **Check-in:** 3:00 PM
- **Check-out:** 10:00 AM
- **Payment:** Deposit due at booking; remaining balance due 21 days prior to arrival
- **Cancellation:**
  - 60+ days before arrival: refund minus $100 fee
  - 21–60 days before arrival: forfeit first installment
  - Less than 21 days before arrival: nonrefundable
- **Pets:** Not allowed during summer season (except ADA-certified service animals); off-peak season with prior approval and a fee
- **Smoking:** Prohibited inside all units; $300 cleaning fee for violations
- **Quiet hours:** 10:00 PM to 8:00 AM
- **Boat slips:** Available to rent for $15 per day
- **Damage deposit:** Refundable damage deposit may be required
- **Linens:** Towels, bed linens, and starter paper products provided; guests should bring extra for longer stays and pool/beach towels
- **Check-in method:** Contactless with unique access code sent before arrival
- **Guest support:** Local team available by phone/text; 24/7 helpline for emergencies

## 7. Location & Getting There

Source: Guesty "Neighborhood" and "Getting Around" descriptions + existing website.

### Location
- Hideaway Resort sits on a quiet peninsula of Table Rock Lake near Galena, Missouri
- Water on three sides — sweeping lake views and tranquil sunsets
- Address: 1715 Hideaway Road, Galena, MO 65656
- Coordinates: approx. -93.499194, 36.663382
- Located at the end of Highway Y on a peninsula off Route 76

### Distances

- Nearby: Hideaway Marina (convenience store, boat rentals)

### Getting Around

- Free parking at all cabins

### Airports

- Springfield-Branson National Airport (SGF)

## 8. Activities & Attractions

Source: Existing website.

### On-Site / Table Rock Lake
- Bass fishing (nationally ranked — Top 100 Bass Lakes)
- Boating and sailing
- Wakeboarding and water skiing
- Paddleboarding
- Swimming
- Cliff diving
- Scenic sunset viewing

### Table Rock Lake Facts
- Over 850 miles of shoreline
- 43,000+ acres of surface area
- Water depths exceeding 200 feet
- Fishable year-round (mild winters)

### Branson, MO (20-30 min drive)
- Silver Dollar City (18 miles)
- 76 Strip (20 miles)
- Live entertainment shows
- Shopping (outlets and craft stores)
- Championship golf courses
- Hiking and biking trails
- Ozark Mountain Christmas (seasonal)
- Resource: ExploreBranson.com

### Boating Regulations
- Missouri boater education law: anyone born after Jan 1, 1984 must pass a course
- Hideaway Marina offers rentals (fishing boats, sport boats, pontoons, paddleboards, jet skis)

### Fishing Licensing
- Missouri: valid license required (exceptions: residents 65+, anyone under 15)
- Arkansas side: similar licensing rules
- Online license acquisition available
